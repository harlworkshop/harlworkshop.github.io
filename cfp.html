Prospective participants in the workshop are required to submit a contribution as:

- Extended abstract (max 2 pages)
- Full paper (max 6 pages)

where both pages limit include references. Submissions must be in PDF following the IEEE conference style in two-columns (also available on Overleaf), and they will follow a single blind review process.

Send your PDF manuscript through EasyChair: here

Selected contributions will be presented during the workshop as online spotlight talks. Accepted papers will be uploaded to the workshop website, but are free to appear in other journals or conference proceedings.

We particularly encourage authors to share their in-progress works in the form of extended abstracts and short papers.
