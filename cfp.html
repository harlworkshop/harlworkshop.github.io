Prospective participants in the workshop are required to submit a contribution as:

- Extended abstract (max 2 pages)
- Full paper (max 6 pages)

Submissions must be in PDF following the IEEE conference style in two-columns.
Send your PDF manuscript through EasyChair: here

Selected contributions will be presented during the workshop as online spotlight talks.

Important dates:

Paper submission deadline: July 02, 2021
Notification of acceptance: July 30, 2021
Camera-ready version: August 12, 2021
Workshop: August 27, 2021

Journal Special Issue:

Contributors to the workshop will be invited to submit extended versions of the manuscripts to a topical collection (special issue) at Neural Computing and Applications.
Submissions will be peer reviewed consistent with the journal practices.
