Prospective participants in the workshop are required to submit a contribution as:

- Extended abstract (max 2 pages)
- Full paper (max 6 pages with no page-limit for references)

Submissions must be in PDF following the IEEE conference style in two-columns (also available on Overleaf), and they will follow a single blind review process.

Send your PDF manuscript through EasyChair: here.

Selected contributions will be presented during the workshop either as oral presentations or in poster sessions. Accepted papers will be uploaded to the workshop website, but are free to appear in other journals or conference proceedings.

Contributors to the workshop will be invited to submit extended versions of the manuscripts to a special issue whose Call for Papers will be appropriately disseminated here.
Submissions will be peer reviewed consistent with the journal practices.
